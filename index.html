Workbook Repository

A collection of useful workbooks made as personal projects. I am completing them one by one.

During 2025 I discovered that I had dangerously high blood pressure. I started taking the prescribed medication, but also decided to change my life style. Eat better, exercise more, and spend less time sitting down. This has opened up a whole new field of interest to me, and one that I would like to share with others. Unhealthy life habits are common in our modern civilization, and they have a serious effect on our lives in many ways. I am writing a series of workbooks to show what can be done.

The material is practical, evidence-informed, and written for people who want clarity rather than slogans. As far as possible I will supply references to support the methods described.
